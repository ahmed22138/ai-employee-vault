## Weekly Audit Process

Every Sunday night at 10 PM, the AI Employee should:

1. **Review Revenue**
   - Total revenue this week
   - Compare to weekly target ($2,500 for $10K/month goal)
   - Identify revenue sources

2. **Check Completed Tasks**
   - Review /Done/ folder for the week
   - Identify bottlenecks or delays
   - Calculate average task completion time

3. **Analyze Subscriptions**
   - Check for unused services
   - Flag cost increases
   - Identify optimization opportunities

4. **Generate CEO Briefing**
   - Create Monday Morning CEO Briefing in /Briefings/
   - Include revenue, completed tasks, bottlenecks, proactive suggestions
   - Highlight upcoming deadlines
